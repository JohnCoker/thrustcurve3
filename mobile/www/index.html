The ThrustCurve to-go app is a companion to the ThrustCurve.org web site, a shared collection of rocket motor data and simulator files.

The goal of this app is to provide a way to take enough information with you to choose which motor to fly in your rocket, even without cell phone coverage at the launch site.

For a richer experience when online, use the web site; it will adapt to the small screen of your phone.
